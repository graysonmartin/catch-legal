Common Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to your profile, tap settings, and select "delete account". This permanently removes your profile, cats, encounters, photos, and social data from our servers.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the menu on any encounter or profile and select "report". You can also block users from their profile page.

Why does the app need my location?

Location is used to tag where you spotted a cat. It's only accessed while the app is open — never in the background. You can deny location access and still use the app, but encounters won't have a pin on the map.

How does breed identification work?

Breed classification runs entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No photos are sent to external servers for analysis.
